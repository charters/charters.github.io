Hoos Hungry

A mobile app for displaying what dining halls are open at UVA.

"What dining halls are open?" - you'd think it'd be an easy question to answer. Unfortunately, UVA's dining hall information,including their hours, is scattered throughout a web of individual dining hall pages. There is a .PDF students can reference, but it lists all the dining hall hours, and you'd have to go through one-by-one.

Using Node.js and Cheerio, an HTML-scraping library, I scraped dining hall hours from their individual pages and populated a personal database with the hours. Built on Scaphold, a GraphQL back-end as a service platform, I built a ReactJS based front-end optimized for mobile. Simple and functional.
